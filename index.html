About Us

Law is there to support justice, but you can fight for justice only when you are well aware of your rights. Our Law has provided equal opportunities & justified rights for everyone & POSHAW takes charge of spreading awareness about the same in an understandable manner. We are an end-to-end compliance provider that helps corporate & non-corporate organizations maintain their workspaces as per the Sexual Harassment of Women (Prevention, Prohibition, & Redressal) Act, 2013. We provide legal aid for the Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace & mentor employers to strengthen their work policies to maintain a PoSH compliant environment
